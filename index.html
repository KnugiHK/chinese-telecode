Chinese-Telecode Converter

Instructions:

  • Enter either Chinese characters (simplified or traditional) or telecode in the input field below.
  • Multiple characters and telecode entries are accepted.
  • For telecode, separate each code with a space.
  • Do not mix Chinese characters and telecode in the same input.
  • Use the toggle switch in the upper right corner to choose between Traditional (TC) and Simplified (SC) Chinese output.
